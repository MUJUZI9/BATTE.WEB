Makerere University Main Library

MISSION:
To meet the study, teaching, research and outreach information needs for sustainable development.

VISION:
A centre of excellence in the provision of library and information services in Africa.

Some of the services provided include;
  • Documentary services
  • Delivery services
  • Binding services
  • Current awareness services
  • Printing services
  • Selective disssemination information services.
The activities done include;
  • Cataloging
  • Classification
  • Circulation
  • Borrowing
  • Serials managemnt
Tools used are; google scholar, e-resources such as, e-journals and e-books, databases. the people who use this library; both internal and external users, libray stuff, technicl stuff, catalogers, classifiers, reference librarians and library support stuff .the main library consists of eight sections.

African/Special collection section.
Collection section is located on the 4th level of the Library. African section materials include books and periodicals related to East Africa, general African books by explorers and travellers plus books and documents published in and about Uganda.

Binary section
It is on the third flow. It provides services to students, staff, the library itself and private customers with fully trained prefessionals who use book binding skills and craftmanship.

Digitazation section
This section is responsible for the work flow processes of turning analog library materials int digital form. The head of digitization unit is Miriam Kakayi

Resources section
These include thesis, dessertations, peer reviwed research and confrence papers. these are all authored by Makerere University researhers. significant audio-visual materails for uganda's heritage are kept there.
