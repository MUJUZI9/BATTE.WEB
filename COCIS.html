ABOUT COCIS

The COCIS library is one of the libraries that is under Makerere University Library. The COCIS library is a reference library and is located on the first level of Block B on the COCIS building. The library opens at 8am in the morning and closes at 7pm in the evening. The books are kept in both closed and open access format.The COCIS library caters for the schools of SCIT (School of Computing and Information Technology and EASLIS (East African School of Library and Information Science). It provides information to courses like:-
  1. Bachelor of Software Engineering
  2. Bachelor of Information Systems Technology
  3. Degree of Doctorate of Philosophy in Information Science.
  4. Master of Science in Information Science
  5. Master of Records and Archives Management
  6. Bachelor of Library and Information Sciences
  7. Bachelor of Records Management

ACTIVITIES CARRIED OUT AT COCIS LIBRARY

  • Accessioning

  • Serial management

  • Generation of reports.
